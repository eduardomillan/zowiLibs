Zowi

Open-source biped robot platform. Programmable, hackable, and fun.

Zowi was born as a toy, but after playing with it, it becomes an educational robotic platform with a long didactic journey. It sparks curiosity about how robots and technological products work — touching on hardware, software, and 3D design.

Zowi is a biped robot capable of dancing, measuring distances, detecting noise, emitting sounds, and displaying expressions on an LED mouth panel. It has a reprogrammable brain board powered by a wireless battery, and communicates via Bluetooth with the ZowiApp.
