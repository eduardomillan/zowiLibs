ZOWI Alarm v2

A security-themed game for Zowi that turns the robot into a motion/noise alarm system and a patrolling guardian. Developed by BQ (Anita de Prado & Jose Alberca, December 2015). Released under GPL.

Overview

Zowi Alarm has 4 modes selected via the back buttons. The robot uses its ultrasonic sensor and noise sensor to detect intruders and trigger an audio-visual alarm.

Modes

ModeNameHow to SelectDescription
0IdleDefault on bootZowi waits. After 80 seconds of inactivity, falls asleep.
1Alarm SystemButton AStationary alarm with 10s countdown and siren.
2Zowi GuardianButton B (or A+B)Alarm + patrol routine every 8 seconds.
4TeleoperationSerial data receivedZowi listens for serial commands.

How to Play

  1. Power on Zowi — it plays a connection melody, shows a "littleUuh" animation, then smiles.
  2. Press a back button to select a mode.
  3. Mode 1 (Alarm System): Zowi starts a 10-second countdown (beeping with a shield symbol). After arming, it monitors distance and noise. If an intruder is detected (noise ≥ 680 or distance closer than baseline), it blasts a loud alternating siren (A5 ↔ A7) with a full-brightness alarm symbol. Press any button to stop.
  4. Mode 2 (Zowi Guardian): Same alarm, but every 8 seconds Zowi performs a patrol routine — looking around with angry expressions and re-calibrating the baseline distance.

Alarm Behavior

Both Mode 1 and Mode 2 share the same alarm logic:

  1. Arming: A 10-second countdown with a shield symbol and a beep every second.
  2. Monitoring: Zowi continuously checks getNoise() >= 680 and getDistance() < initDistance.
  3. Trigger: When either condition is met, Zowi enters an alarm loop showing a full-brightness alarm symbol and playing an alternating siren (A5 ↔ A7, 880 Hz ↔ 3520 Hz) until a button is pressed.

Key Functions

ZowiArmingAlarmSystem()

A 10-second countdown before the alarm activates. During the countdown, Zowi shows a shield symbol and plays a short beep every second. After the countdown, it records the baseline distance and activates the alarm.

ZowiGuardian()

A patrol routine called every 8 seconds in Mode 2. Zowi shows surprise, plays a cuddly sound, shows an angry mouth, trembles 4 times, looks left and right, then smiles. After patrolling, it re-calibrates the baseline distance.
